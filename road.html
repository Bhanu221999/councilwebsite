Roadworks

We carry out roadworks to repair and improve the roads in Walsall Borough. This may involve road closures and diversions, or the use of temporary traffic lights.

Public utility companies also carry out roadworks to maintain essential gas, water, electricity and phone services. If there is an emergency, they can close a road up to two hours before letting us know about it.
