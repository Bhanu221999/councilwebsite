How to book a repair and re-schedule an appointment

This service is only available for new non-emergency repairs.
To book a new repair or re-schedule an existing appointment online, you need to sign in to or create a BRUM account.
Once you have signed in, you will arrive at your account page. Scroll down until you see the 'Housing' section and select 'Other housing online services'.
