How to report a problem with a road or footpath

You can use our online reporting form to tell us about the following problems on roads and footpaths anywhere in Walsall Borough:

  • potholes
  • blocked road drains (find out what to do if you have a flood on your property)
  • missing or damaged manhole covers (find out who is responsible if it's on your property)
  • road and pavement defects (for example, broken slabs or kerbs)
  • faded road markings
  • missing or damaged road signs
  • skips and scaffolding
  • traffic calming
